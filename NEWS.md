# blueterra 0.1.0

* Initial development version.
* Added raster input, validation, preparation, terrain metrics, process groups,
  transects, isobath corridors, summaries, modeling helpers, plotting helpers,
  synthetic example data, tests, README, and vignettes.
* Added reduced real bathymetry examples from the southwest Puerto Rico shelf
  margin near La Parguera, Puerto Rico, plus sampling rectangles.
* Improved hillshade-supported terrain maps, site-location documentation, and
  scientific examples for README, vignettes, and visual proof.
